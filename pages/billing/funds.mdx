# Manage Funds on Web3 Wallet Payment Method

<Callout type="warning">
  Manage Funds is not applicable for Solana.
</Callout>

You need to add a [Web3 Payment Method](/billing/payment-method/) before managing funds in your Spheron escrow wallet.

## Deposit Funds

To ensure you have sufficient funds to Top up your compute balance or pay for your storage subscription, \
you need to deposit funds to the Spheron escrow wallet.

**Follow these steps to deposit funds:**
1. Connect your Web3 Wallet, if it is not already connected.
2. Select **Deposit Funds** from the **Payment Method** dropdown.
3. Add the amount you want to deposit and Click "Deposit" to approve and deposit your funds.

## Withdraw Funds

Spheron allows you to withdraw funds from the Spheron escrow wallet. You can do this anytime. \

**Follow these steps to withdraw funds:**

1. Connect your Web3 Wallet, if it is not already connected.
2. Select **Withdraw Funds** from the **Payment Method** dropdown.
3. Enter the amount you want to withdraw and Click "Withdraw."

<Callout type="info">
  **NOTE:** \
  An insufficient balance in the Spheron escrow wallet may stop all
  the running services. You can find the details of all the deposit and
  withdrawal transactions in the **Payment History** section under the **Billing** tab.
</Callout>
