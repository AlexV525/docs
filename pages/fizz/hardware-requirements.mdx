# Fizz Hardware Requirements and Recommendations

## Software Recommendation

Fizz nodes are supported on **MacOS and Linux**, with Windows support coming soon. Your experience may vary depending on the operating system used.

## Fizz Node Requirements

### Minimum Specs
- 2 CPU
- 4 GB RAM
- 20 GB disk

### Recommended Specs
- 4 CPU
- 8 GB RAM
- 100 GB disk

### Recommendations

- The more resources you have, the better, especially if your goal is to maximize performance.
- Any consumer-grade GPU works for Fizz nodes. The supported list is the same as what we have in GPU tiering.

<Callout type="info">
  **NOTE:** For optimal performance, we recommend using hardware that meets or exceeds the recommended specifications.
</Callout>
