# View Compute Plan Usage

You can view your monthly Spheron Compute plan usage on the billing compute usage page.

**This page will break down your compute usage costs for each Cluster showing:**
1. Total Spend in USD
2. Monthly Usage in USD

You can also drill down into each cluster and see the full breakdown on each instance's total usage and monthly cost.
You can use this page to forecast monthly compute costs.

<Callout type="info">
    You need to ensure you have enough funds in your compute balance to cover future usage costs for all your clusters to ensure uninterrupted service.

    Setting an automatic top up is the best way to ensure uninterrupted service for all your clusters.
</Callout>
